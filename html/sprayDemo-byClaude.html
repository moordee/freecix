How to Implement

Here's the HTML structure used in these examples:

<section class="spray-layer-container">
  
  <!-- Spray Layer 1 -->
  <div class="spray-layer spray-layer-1 
              spray-layer-top-right 
              spray-layer-lg"></div>
  
  <!-- Spray Layer 2 -->
  <div class="spray-layer spray-layer-2 
              spray-layer-bottom-left"></div>
  
  <!-- Your Content -->
  <div class="container spray-content">
    <h1>Your Content Here</h1>
  </div>
  
</section>

Key Classes:

Container: spray-layer-container
Layer: spray-layer
Content: spray-content
Position: spray-layer-[position]
